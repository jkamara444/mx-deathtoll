LATEST VICTIM

Carlos Castro

Crime Reporter, Codigo Norte Veracruz

January 2026

Gunmen in eastern Mexico killed journalist Carlos Castro in early January, adding to the rising number of press killings in one of the most dangerous countries for journalists.

Castro, who reported on crime for Código Norte Veracruz, was shot at a restaurant in Poza Rica, Veracruz, according to the State Commission for the Attention and Protection of Journalists and the digital outlet RECORD. Authorities have not made any arrests.
